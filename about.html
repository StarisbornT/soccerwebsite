JUNIOR STAR SOCCER, has come to end frustration, depression and
crime from our community and society at large.

1

SCREENING EXERCISE

In this process, the Junior Star Soccer team, will be able to select the good players, both grassroots and professionals side, to face the second phase of JSS. The screening exercise, will be carried out by our European top scout expert, to ensure credibility to her European top clubs allied.

2

FOOTBALL TOURNAMENT

In this process, the JSS team will then proceed to showcasing the selected players from the screened exercise by her experts (foreign coaches) to the European top club's allied, with a view of getting them signed. Where they leave the shores of Nigeria with a working contract as to maintain their dignity, honour and honesty.

3

REPRESENTATION

In this process, Junior Star Soccer legal representatives, shall take upon themselves to represent all her players in accordance with the provision of FIFA with the view to promote , showcase , negotiate and renegotiate the players rights and best interest to the World of soccer globally. This negotiation is not and will not be limited to the male gender alone, because we believe all BORN are BORN EQUAL.
